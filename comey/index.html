Analysis

Overall, there was not a lot that surprised me when looking at the frequency distributions of Comey's opening statement. Given the nature of the situation, we would expect to see words like President, intelligence, and conversation throughout the text.

However, there are two tokens here that warrant some additional examination, leadership and personally, because of their specific context within the statement.

The word Leadership appears nine times throughout the statement. While high enough to make the top fifty tokens, it is not anywhere near the top of the list. However, this word characterizes Comey's recount of these matters and bolsters his credibility as the by-the-books law man. All nine of those occurrences reference Comey's strides to involve FBI or other DOJ leadership in the intricacies of the investigation and his dealings with Trump. I would expect to see this theme played out in the open session.

The other word that stuck out to me is Personally. At first glance, you might expect this word to be used as an adverbial qualifier for Comey's own recollections, as in a "Personally, I don't think..." sense. Yet, that is not the case. Personally here refers to Trump's concern with the distinction between an investigation into him, personally, or just his campaign. Again, I'd expect to see this play out in some fashion as well.
